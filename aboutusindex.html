The Queen City Watering Hole's misssion is to help Charlotte residents and newcomers alike find their new favorite watering hole and grow their personal and professional networks the old fashion way; by raising a glass. This application will allow users to locate a brewery based on their current location. If the user is having a hard time choose a place, the app can provide a random brewery to be chosen by the click of a button.
